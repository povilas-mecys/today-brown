About Today@Brown

Today@Brown is a digest of announcements and events from across the University delivered to all campus inboxes daily and integrated with the accompanying Today@Brown website and Events@Brown calendar.

Today@Brown Announcements Events@Brown Calendars

An official communications platform for the Brown community, Today@Brown is:

The go-to source for announcements from University leaders about important initiatives, policies, deadlines, opportunities, budget and funding updates and much more.

A moderated digest of announcements and events submitted by Brown faculty, staff and student organizations.

The best communication tool for reaching large, broad audiences within Brown, and one that’s highly segmented — 18 different versions of Today@Brown go to audience segments grouped by student or employee type, class year, area of study and other characteristics.

Interest categories

By selecting their interest categories, users can personalize Today@Brown to highlight relevant announcements or events within the digests they receive daily.

Priority Items

Priority Items are important campus messages that are crucial for operations or sharing updates on significant University initiatives. Only a small number of designated offices have permissions to send Priority Items, which have replaced most all-campus emails previously sent by the Office of the President, Office of the Provost, Dean of the College, University Human Resources, and other key academic and administrative units.

The Today@Brown subject line indicates when there are Priority Items in the email digest so readers can easily spot important announcements in their inboxes. And Priority Items run at the top of the email digest so they are always immediately viewable.

Today@Brown Readership

Curious about whether people actually read Today@Brown? The answer is a resounding yes.

User testing has shown that Today@Brown has higher open rates compared to all-community email messages sent directly to inboxes. Additionally, the Today@Brown website receives over one million pageviews annually.

And mass emails on campus have declined more than 90% since Today@Brown’s launch in 2017, enabling faculty, staff and students to focus on their work and studies.

Today@Brown is now fully integrated with Events@Brown.
